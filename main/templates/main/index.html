Welcome to scien-Z.com !

We share articles on the most exciting topics of modern science in a way that everyone can understand. Prequisite knowledge on every topic can be found is you click on the arrow or swipe to the right.


Everyone will find their level

Please, enjoy the articles broken down into comrehensible pieces. Just press the buttoms to the left and to the right in each of the color chunks The vision for the project is to create a system that allows to take any scientific article and break it down to prequisites so that one could understand the topic regardless of their level The system will work like that:

  • 1. You log into your account
  • 2. You upload an article, or a textbook extract, and the system links them to the pieces of information involved
  • 3. You can go as far down the rabbit hole ass you like and as you need
  • 4. If you have the rights, you will be able to post the article on the website, or even add the prequisite blocks into the system
We aim to make science more accessible and understandable for everyone. Please note that the project is still in the development stage and the interface is not ready for usage just yet. Sorry bout that( In the meanwhile, enjoy the articles that we already have on our pages!
